About Me

Charlee was adopted from Hangzhou, China when she was 10 months old. She grew up in Lynbrook, New York. She graduated from Brandeis University Cum Laude with a B.A. in Psychology and Music. She gained some experience in Python by working in the Neurochemistry and Cognition Lab at Brandeis University. There she developed the group's behavioral tests using PsychoPy; these tests were both used in-person and pushed online thorugh GitHub and Pavlovia. After leaving the lab, she enrolled as a full time student in The Georgia Institute of Technology's Full Stack Coding BootCamp to further her skills. In her spare time she enjoys experimenting in the kitchen and playing the flute. While she works, she listens to Animal Crossing Music to get her through the day.
